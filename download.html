Get Liker Extension

To download and install the Liker Extension, follow these steps:
  1. Click on the button below.
  2. Unzip the file.
  3. In the address bar, type chrome://extensions.
  4. Enable "Developer Mode" by toggling the switch located in the upper-right corner.
  5. Click on the "Load unpacked" button.
  6. Select the directory where you downloaded and zipped the file.
  7. Enter to WhatsApp Web
  8. Enable the liker extension (pluzzle like button near the url bar)
  9. Refresh
  10. Choose a group and scroll through the group's messages.
  11. Choose how many days back you want to like posts from.
  12. Sit back and let the extension automatically like the posts for you - it may take a few minutes depending on the number of posts.
